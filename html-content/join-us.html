ODI Events for Members

We are running three events each year for ODI incubatees, associates and members:

Networking Events

We run two evening networking events each year, where you can meet others in the ODI community over drinks and canapes.

Annual Summit

The annual summit will be held in October and have a full programme of talks and workshops. There will be a gala dinner open only to members with a well known speaker.

ODI's Space

The ODI has a great office space where you are bound to bump in to others who share your interest in open data.

As part of our community, and subject to availability, you can book hot desks, and use our desks, pods, meeting space.

Please note that meeting room allowances do not include use of the boardroom.

Sponsorship Opportunities

As a sponsor, you will be acknowledged for your sponsorship of the events that we run in your sector: both community events and our innovation days.

Platinum sponsors gain additional opportunities to advertise to potential customers, including:

  • guest speaker at our member events
  • exhibitor stand during our annual summit
  • opportunities to distribute literature

Sector Pioneers Board

As an ODI Partner, we recognise your leading contribution to the adoption of open data in your sector.

The Sector Pioneers Board will give you the opportunity to mix with similar thought leaders in other fields and provide direct feedback to the ODI Board on the areas that make a difference to you.

Discount On Services

The ODI has a number of commercial services where you will receive a 20% discount as a member:

  • Training - including a bespoke courses for your organisation
  • Professional Services - we provide a full suite of services
  • Research
